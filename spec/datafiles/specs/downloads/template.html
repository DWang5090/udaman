UHERO State Forecast Update:
Some Improvement, After Disappointing 2011

Hawaii’s stop-start recovery continued in the fourth quarter. We are a bit more optimistic about prospects for 2012.

A complete report is attached in Adobe Acrobat pdf format. A brief summary will be released to the public on Friday, February 10. 

As a service to UHERO sponsors, we are also happy to provide the attached file containing the extended five-year UHERO forecast in Excel spreadsheet form. You may find this useful for preparing reports for in-house use.

The UHERO Forecast Project

UHERO's Forecast Project is a community-sponsored research program of the University of Hawai'i at Manoa. The Forecast Project provides the Hawaii community with analysis on economic, demographic, and business trends in the State and the Asia-Pacific region. Summaries and analyses are disseminated through forecast reports, public presentations, and regular forecast updates. UHERO develops and maintains high-frequency industry level statistical models of Hawaii, its four counties and key external economies to support rigorous forecasting exercises.

All sponsors receive the full schedule of UHERO reports with extended five-year forecast tables, as well as other benefits that vary with the level of financial commitment.
